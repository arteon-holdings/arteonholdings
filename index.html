Some businesses are built to be sold. Others are built because someone saw a need, filled it well, and kept going — through difficulty, through change, through years of quiet persistence. We are interested in the second kind.

WHAT WE LOOK FOR

A business with real foundations.

We look for well-run B2B businesses with genuine client relationships, good people, and an owner who built something worth continuing. Not the loudest or the fastest-growing. The ones with depth.

We are drawn to businesses where the value lies in knowledge, relationships, and craft — where what has been built is not easily replicated, and where the right leadership can quietly unlock what was always there.

An owner ready for the next chapter.

The most important conversations are not about price. They are about what happens to the people, the clients, and the culture that someone spent years building.

We take time to understand what matters most to the people who made a business what it is. The transition is shaped around them — not around a template.
